Getting around the city

Easiest way to move in-between the city’s districts is to either take SKM, which is undoubtedly the fastest means of transport. It can take you within minutes from the heart of the Old Town to the famous Gdańsk Shipyard, where ECS is located. SKM leads nextly to the freshly re-discovered district known for its cafes, art venues and shops - Wrzeszcz. Another very popular means of transport in Gdańsk, trams, despite being a bit slower, are much more direct since there are 11 lines altogether. Buses are another way to get around the city too. A great mobile app called Jakdojade may come in handy since it plans the whole trip for you
